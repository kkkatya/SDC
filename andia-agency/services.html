Dear applicant!

If you decide to go to college named Suleyman Demirel, please note the following information:

In our college accepted students with basic secondary education.

Education is conducted in Kazakh, Russian, English and Turkish.

Today may employers impose knowledge of English language to specialists. Given the demands of employers, the college planned maintenance of 60% of general and special subjects in English. Including general subjects such as mathematics, computer science, physics, chemistry, biology are taught in English. Accordingly is conducted an in-depth learning English language.

Along with the Kazakh teachers work teachers from Turkey and Philippines. In all professions is learned a second foreign language - Turkish.

Created all necessary conditions for various research, educational work, which in turn contributes to the development of skills needed in school and after school.

Each year, updated and improved the material and technical base of the college. There are lectures and laboratory halls, computer rooms, a library, a reading room, a spacious dining room, medical center. Our aim - to help young people find their way in life, and determine the right career choice. Pedagogical staff of the college is making every effort to have YOUR student's life was a successful, so you are here to get true friends.

The main directions of the college:

  • quality education;
  • friendly psychological atmosphere;
  • team building;
  • promotion of healthy lifestyles.
