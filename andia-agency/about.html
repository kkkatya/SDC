About College of Suleyman Demirel

Suleyman Demirel College was found in 2011 by the Kazakh Turkish Education Foundation (KATEV), Suleyman Demirel College is a privately supported, co-educational institution with a worldwide mission. Its major commitments are quality programs of pre-undergraduate instruction, research and public service provided at the most reasonable cost to students.

The College's programs are particularly responsive to the needs of individuals, public groups, and agencies in the community; all are rapidly adjusting themselves to the quickly changing social, political and economic environment of the country.

The College aims to develop and pursue its programs in a manner to ensure that eligible students who desire to develop and expand their scholastic skills for personal, occupational, or professional growth have the opportunity to do so regardless of socio-economic status. Respect for the intellectual potential and dignity of students as individual human beings without regard for racial, ethnic, or cultural background, shall be paramount.

The College is able to hold more than 500 students. There are 7 departments, 19 auditoriums, 1 conference hall, 2 computer labs, 1 research centers, a medical center, 2 dormitories and 1 comprehensive library accessible to all students, faculty and staff. In addition, the campus also houses 1 canteen to provide a relaxing and social atmosphere. Whole territory of the College is covered by free Wi-Fi connection for students to help in their researches.

Suleyman Demirel College provides education in four languages: Kazakh, English, Russian and Turkish. The College prepares pre-undergraduates in 7 branches:

  • Computer Science and Software Engineering

  • Translation Studies

  • Economics

  • Finance

  • Accounting and Audit

  • Management

  • Marketing

The College exists to provide an education for students from around the Kazakhstan that equips them with the knowledge and skills they require to pursue their ambitions. We seek to seek to create a learning environment that promotes free thinking, exchange of ideas, innovation and critical reflection among students.

We passionately deliver an education according to Ministry of Education Standards and living experience which is student focused. We provide conditions to ignite their intellectual growth, to install in them a sense of citizenship and to positively influence the larger educational community. We celebrate diversity in cultures. We foster close relationship among students and teachers. We encourage the practices of personal reflection and intensive exploration of the market place through research and experience believing that career satisfaction is born of an authentic understanding of the self and the needs of the world.
